The Homecoming

A short sound story and visual story about the contrast between warm memories of the childhood and grey routine of the reality

REMINISCENCE OF THE PAST
I miss my childhood. It has been years since I last visited my hometown, but I clearly remember the warm feeling... I can even recall the specific parts of the image that lives in my heart

I remember the sunsets. The most beautiful sunsets in the world I used to watch from my house's window everyday

I remember my grandparents' cows walking along the fields of the greenest grass

I remember my childhood friends' laugh and playful carelessness

MORNING ROUTINE
It was merely a dream... I live a completely different life now

This repetitive sound of water is so depressing. Every single morning starts with looking at this old sink...

I despise the taste of this cheap bread. Yet, living in the city is so expensive I can barely afford anything else for breakfast...

Despite my hatred towards this city, it's time to dress up and rush to the office

THE ROAD TO WORK

The city never stops moving. Thousands of people trapped in the same never-ending cycle. It's getting louder every day.

In the sudden quiet, the traffic feels miles away. But I can't erase the noise completely, I'm just delaying it. I still have to get to work.

THE TRAIN
THE HOMECOMING

I...I am finally back...It's been so long, but I finally reached the place that was haunting me in my dreams...

The nature is the same, but I don't see any farm animals walking under the sunshine anymore. I don't hear kids laughing and playing. Time flies so fast, and so much has changed

Alas, it is impossible to repeat the past. But the memories and the feeling of my childhood will stay with me forever, reminding me who I am even in the toughest times.
